\section{Verification}
\label{sec:verification}

We apply the framework's own verification mechanism to the 34-step
derivation (Phases A--I, Steps 0--33).  The three-point audit tests each step for:
(1)~\emph{Hidden Chooser}---no alternative A0$^*$-compliant continuation exists;
(2)~\emph{Encoding Invariance}---re-executing under different encoding
conventions produces the same quotient structure;
(3)~\emph{Distinguishability Equivalence}---every distinction introduced is
witnessed, and no witnessed distinction is omitted.
A fourth check verifies algebraic properties on finite models via Z3.
The protocol is depicted in \cref{fig:audit-flowchart}.

Table~\ref{tab:audit-results} confirms: all 34 steps pass the three-point
audit.  Twenty steps have algebraic properties checked on finite models via
Z3 (Appendix~\ref{app:z3}).  All steps are fully forced by A0$^*$---no
modeling commitments remain.

\textbf{Lean~4 verification:} The full derivation, quantitative seed,
manifestability block, and P~$=$~NP chain are verified across 140 Lean~4
files (zero sorry, zero admit, one axiom A0$^*$).  The manifestability
extension adds $\chi(W)$, the refinement kernel, the value equation,
and the Cook--Levin route to P~$=$~NP
(see \cref{sec:manifestability} and Appendix~\ref{app:theorem-table}).
