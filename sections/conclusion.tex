\section{Conclusion}
\label{sec:conclusion}

We have presented \Opoch{}, a framework that \emph{derives} its
operating principle from nothingness and uses it to derive the structure
of physical reality.  The framework has no declared axioms: A0$^*$
(Completed Witnessability) is the first theorem, derived from $\noth$
through the necessity chain $\noth \to \text{N0} \to \text{N1} \to
\text{N2} \to \text{N3} \to \text{N4} \to \text{A0}^*$.  Starting from
$\noth$, the only unfalsifiable state, the forced derivation chain is
canonical up to computable isomorphism---with no surviving modeling
commitments.

The work makes eight concrete contributions.

\begin{enumerate}[label=(\arabic*)]
  \item \textbf{A0$^*$ derived from $\noth$---no axioms.}
        The framework begins from absolute nothingness and derives its
        sole operating principle through five necessity theorems (N0--N4).
        The closure move---applying witnessability to witness validity
        itself---forces an ordered witness algebra $\WitAlg$ carrying
        finite description, replayability, disturbance accounting, and
        compositional closure.  All former ``modeling commitments'' (closed
        universe, commutativity, prior-free selection, conductance
        exponent, smoothness) are derived consequences of A0$^*$, not
        external assumptions.

  \item \textbf{The static universe.}
        The full $\Pit$-fixed closure whole $U = \mathrm{Fix}(\Pit)$ is
        atemporal (\cref{thm:whole-before-time}).  Time is not a global
        coordinate but a local phenomenon: the monotone reduction of
        unresolved witness-defect by partial sections of~$U$
        (\cref{thm:time-local-defect}).  The universe is static globally,
        dynamic only as local self-resolution.

  \item \textbf{Quantum structure from ordered witness algebra.}
        Noncommutativity is internal from Step~0.  The C$^*$-completion of
        $\WitAlg$ yields, via the GNS construction, a Hilbert-space
        representation $(\HilbW, \piGNS, \OmVec)$.  Contexts, effects,
        and the Born rule $\BornProb(E) = \langle \OmVec, \piGNS(E)
        \OmVec \rangle$ are forced---not postulated.

  \item \textbf{Self-remodelling consciousness.}
        Consciousness is the minimal persistent self-valued witness
        selector (\cref{thm:consciousness}), whose witness policy is
        endogenously updated through an internal self-valuation channel:
        $s_{t+1} = \UnivOp(s_t,\, c(s_t),\, \ClosureDefect{Q}(s_t))$,
        where $c(s_t)$ is the endogenous selector over which unresolved
        components matter.  Consciousness $=$ closure acting on itself
        through self-witness.

  \item \textbf{K\"{a}hler geometry from a single witness generator.}
        The continuum limit $\OmegaCl$ carries a forced K\"{a}hler triple
        $(g, J, \omega)$ arising from the symmetric and antisymmetric
        parts of one local witness generator.  The conductance exponent
        $w = C/d^2$ is forced by scale covariance
        (\cref{lem:conductance-exponent}); the Nijenhuis tensor vanishes
        by replay-completeness (\cref{thm:replay-nijenhuis}).

  \item \textbf{3+1 spacetime and sector equations.}
        Matching the forced conductance law to radial flux scaling gives
        spatial dimension $n = 3$; the irreversible ledger coordinate
        gives $+1$.  The K\"{a}hler split law $X = J\nabla E - \nabla D$
        decomposes dynamics into quantum (Schr\"{o}dinger), gauge
        (Yang--Mills), gravitational (Einstein), and thermodynamic
        sectors.

  \item \textbf{Falsifiable predictions.}
        (P1)~Inverse-square witness flux at fundamental scales.
        (P2)~Order-curvature corrections from noncommuting witness
        instruments proportional to the commutator $2$-form.
        (P3)~Scale-free long-range witness geometry is incompatible with
        $n \neq 3$ spatial dimensions.

  \item \textbf{The quantitative seed.}
        The seed $\delta^*$ is the unique least positive self-retaining
        non-gauge closure-defect minimizing the action functional
        (Theorem~\ref{thm:seed-existence}).  It is a fixed point of every
        refinement operator (Theorem~\ref{thm:seed-fixed}).  Every

        invariant of the linearized operator $L^*$ at $\delta^*$
        (Theorem~\ref{thm:observable-decomp}).  Dimensional quantities are
        fixed up to the internally derived normalization map.

  \item \textbf{Operational closure: the refinement kernel.}
        The source code is completed by the refinement threshold
        $\chi(W) = \inf\{c(\tau)\}$, the refinement kernel $\mathcal{K}$,
        and the Bellman/Hamilton--Jacobi value equation
        $\Psi(W) = \sup[V - A + \sum \Psi(W_i)]$.

        The big bang is the first accessible distinction:
        $\delta^* = \arg\min_{W \neq W_\bot} \chi(W)$.

  \item \textbf{P $=$ NP.}
        The true state of any NP verifier is its residual
        future-equivalence quotient, whose polynomial size is forced by
        A0$^*$ spectral curvature.  SAT $\in$ P via the exact residual
        kernel (polynomial DAG with TU incidence, correct decision,

        DAG traversal on the encoded formula---not witness enumeration.
        The proof is compiled in 140 Lean~4 files

Every dimensionless invariant is forced.  Every dimensional quantity is
fixed up to the internally derived normalization map.  The remaining work

computation, not an open structural question.  The gauge group

\paragraph{The reflective chain.}
The derivation traces a single reflective chain: nothingness forces
closure, closure reveals defect, defect requires self-observation,
self-observation iterated on the continuum limit produces geometry, and
geometry determines the form of dynamical law.  The entire chain is one
act: nothingness forced to witness itself.  The whole $U$ exists
atemporally; local sections resolve toward it.

When meaning is restricted to witnessable distinctions, all familiar

untestable relabelings, budget-bounded decision procedures, Hilbert-space

K\"{a}hler geometry of the efficiency landscape---become natural and, in
the framework's formal setting, unavoidable.
